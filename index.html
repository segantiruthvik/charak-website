Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the sessions

‚ùì Do I need prior yoga experience?

Not at all! My sessions are designed for all levels ‚Äì from complete beginners to experienced practitioners. I'll customize the practice based on your current level and goals.

‚ùì What equipment do I need?

Just a yoga mat and comfortable clothing. If you don't have a mat, a soft carpet or towel works too. I'll guide you on any additional props (like blocks or straps) if needed, but household items can substitute.

‚ùì How are sessions conducted?

All sessions are conducted online via Zoom or Google Meet. You'll receive a video link before each session. I'll be able to see you and provide real-time guidance and corrections.

‚ùì Can I book sessions for specific health issues?

Absolutely! The 1-on-1 Rehab sessions are specifically designed for addressing back pain, neck pain, posture issues, and other health concerns. During the trial, we'll discuss your specific needs and create a targeted plan.

‚ùì What's your cancellation policy?

Please provide at least 6 hours notice if you need to reschedule. For session packs, you can reschedule within the validity period. The trial session requires 24 hours notice.

‚ùì How long are the session packs valid?

4-session packs are valid for 8 weeks from the date of first session. This gives you flexibility while maintaining consistency in your practice.
